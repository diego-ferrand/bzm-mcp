# Onboarding

## BlazeMeter University Onboarding FAQ

Navigate BlazeMeter University onboarding process, including SSO login, account registration, and course enrollment.

**Use when**: Navigating BlazeMeter University onboarding process or setting up SSO login, account registration, and course enrollment.

### Overview

[BlazeMeter University](https://university.blazemeter.com/) has moved to a new learning platform. For further assistance with your migrated account, feel free to contact us at [Education.Help@Perforce.com](mailto:Education.Help@Perforce.com).

### How do I sign in with BlazeMeter SSO?

Are you a returning BlazeMeter user?

1. Log on to [https://a.blazemeter.com](https://a.blazemeter.com/)
2. Open the Help Center
3. Click the BlazeMeter University button
4. On the university page, click **Sign In**, then click **Single-Sign On** in the dialog
5. Select a course from the list and click **Enroll** to start learning!

### How to log on for the first time?

Navigate to [university.blazemeter.com](https://university.blazemeter.com). You see the list of available courses.

- **If you have neither a Perforce Learning nor a BlazeMeter account yet**: Click **Register** and enter your email address and password to register
- **If you have a Perforce Learning account from another Perforce product**: Log on through that Perforce product's learning portal. You will have access to BlazeMeter trainings as well
- **If you are a returning BlazeMeter user**: Click **Sign in**. Click **Single Sign-On**. Select a course from the list and click **Enroll** to start learning!

### How do I sign into my existing account?

If you have self-registered in the old BlazeMeter University, first, reset your password. Wait for the reset email and then log in with your email address and your new password.

A quick [video tutorial](https://university.blazemeter.com/learn/courses/527/introduction-to-blazemeters-new-learning-experience) is available to guide returning users through the first sign-in process.

### How do I register a new account?

If you do not have an account in BlazeMeter University already, register a new account.

### What happened to my in-progress or incomplete courses?

Courses that were in progress have been reset on the new platform, so you'll need to restart these from the beginning.

### Where did the subportals go?

The subportals are no longer available separately. All your courses and past achievements can be found in the new portal.

## BlazeMeter University

Learn more about the BlazeMeter continuous testing platform and open-source performance test tools with courses available at BlazeMeter University. We have various courses for all user levels, from general overview to deep dive. You can take an exam at the end of many courses to receive a Certificate of completion and a virtual badge.

**Use when**: Learning about BlazeMeter, accessing training courses, or obtaining certifications for BlazeMeter and open-source testing tools.

### How to Log On to the Learning Portal

1. Navigate to [university.blazemeter.com](https://university.blazemeter.com)
2. Click **Sign in**
3. Click **Single Sign-On** and use BlazeMeter SSO
4. Select a course from the list and click **Enroll** to start learning!

### How to Navigate the Learning Portal

**User menu navigation**:
- To start a new course, open the User menu, and select **BlazeMeter University** or another portal
- To return to a course that's in progress or completed, open the User menu, and select **My Courses and Learning Plans**
- To track your progress, certificates, and highest scores, open the User menu, and select **My Activities**
- To track your learning badges, open the User menu, and select **BlazeMeter Badges**

### How to Download Certificates

To download your certificates, open the User menu, and select **My Activities**, then select **Courses**, and click the certificate button for the course.

### Where Can I Find My Achievements History?

Your achievement history has been fully migrated. All your points and badges are now accessible on the new platform. Once you sign in to the new platform, click the **User Menu** button in the top left corner.

- You will find your existing badges and points under **BlazeMeter Badges**
- You find your course history and certificates under **My Activities > Courses**

### How Do I Share Certificates on Social Media?

You can share awarded course badges on Facebook and LinkedIn or copy the link on your **My Badges and Points** page.
